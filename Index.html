I'm Shaheem Salie — a Brand & Graphic Designer based in Cape Town with a sharp eye for visual storytelling, brand consistency, and campaign design that connects.

My work spans the full spectrum of brand communication — from the first logo concept to print-ready production files, social media campaigns to complete brand identity systems. I understand how a brand needs to perform across every touchpoint, both digital and physical.

Skilled in Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, and Canva, I bring creative instinct and technical precision to every project — ensuring designs don't just look good, they work.
